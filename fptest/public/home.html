Mentor Carol P

I feel honored and priviledged to be providing mentorship service to the tech community as a Mentor. I didn't have a mentor when I graduated from my bootcamp and would have loved to have one for support and best paractices. Someone who would have been able to provide guidance on industry culture and general expectations. I plan to offer my mentees all this and more. I think its especially important to have women mentors in a field that is predominantly male. Mentorship for women in this field is especially importanat for retention, as women cite isolation as a main cause for leaving tech. With a male majority, often women feel isolated, and sometimes end up being the only woman on their team and sometime the only woman in technology in their company! I look forward to being there for other women "newbies" starting out in this great and innovative field and look forward to seeing my mentees excel and exceed their goals. Sign up as a mentor and lets play it forward!
